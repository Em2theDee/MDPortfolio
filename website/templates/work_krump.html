WE ARE KRUMPIN'
Motion Graphic
Overview

WE ARE KRUMPIN'! is a visually compelling stop-motion project that authentically captures the essence of Krump culture. Through meticulous stop-motion techniques, the project vividly portrays the dynamic movements and intense expressions synonymous with Krump dance. Its immersive visuals serve to promote an upcoming event at Lavender Hill, teasing viewers with a taste of the raw energy and emotion they can expect to experience firsthand.
